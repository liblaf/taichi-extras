## Pre-Processing

- import PLY to Blender
- move model to origin
  - `RMB` > "Set Origin" > "Origin to Center of Mass"
  - `RMB` > "Snap" > "Selection to Cursor"
- remesh
  - "Properties" `Shift F7` > "Modifiers" > "Add Modifier" > "Remesh"
    - "Smooth"
    - increase "Octree Depth"
- export as ASCII PLY
